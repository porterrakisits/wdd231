Project Siteplan

site name

Roadrunner Skim Co.

This name represents the local area and popular local sport of skimboarding

site purpose

The subject for my project will be the website for a small company that sells skimboards in the St. George area. It will have a landing page that talks about the company, a shop page with a display of the products sold, and a contact page to reach out to the company. The purpose is to create a user-friendly website that boosts sales, engages customers, and simplifies contact with the company.

scenarios

Q: What kind of skimboard is best for beginners?

A: This question helps guide content around product recommendations, explaining the differences between boards and providing tips for newcomers to the sport.

Q: Where are the best skimboarding spots in St. George?

A: This scenario targets the local community, helping visitors find the best places to skimboard in the area, potentially leading to blog content or a local events section.
