About Us

Roadrunner Skim Co. is a St. George, Utah-based company focused on creating high-quality skimboards for riders who want to make the most of every wave and shoreline. We’re passionate about providing durable, performance-driven boards for all skill levels. Whether you're just getting started or you're a seasoned pro, we're here to help you get out there and enjoy the ride. Built for the unique terrain of the Southwest, our boards are designed to handle everything. At Roadrunner, it’s all about the experience – getting outside, having fun, and pushing yourself to the next level.
